Café Harepus

THE KIND BEAN TOUR

The Kind Bean Tour is a project that showcases the “kindest” cafes of Aarhus regarding their values towards animals and the planet. Today we are showcasing the lovely Café Harepus. Named after a baker that fed all the forest animals from a danish children's book called “The Animals of Hakkebakkeskoven”. Another vegan gem hiding in plain sight - in the center of Aarhus. They have a bakery in the basement - where the magic happens and cozy little cafe on ground floor. Don’t miss out on this one.

Food
It is so hard to come by vegan pastry in Denmark but for all of the vegan pastry lovers out there - this one here is your gonna be your hero. If you still haven’t been there, don’t walk, run. This is the highlight of what the café has to offer. Filled croissants, pain un chocolate, seasonal pastries, variety of cakes and in the summer there is even vegan ice cream! The price is like every other local bakery so at least they don’t overcharge for making it plant-based! And on top of that they make cakes to order for anyone who is interested. They get 5/5 kind beans!

Coffee
Quite important this one. But there is not that much to say about it, because it’s great! You won’t be disappointed from the quality of the Copenhagen Coffeelab coffee beans, which are by the way Fair Trade certified and organic. They have all the coffee you need and on top of that you can get a matcha and some Østerlandsk Thehus tea, if you don’t drink coffee. That gets them 5/5 kind beans.

Atmosphere
on the walls. It has quiet backyard that is especially nice in the summer. They have music playing and employees are always kind and welcoming. It’s a perfect place to come and meet with a friend over a cup of coffee and a piece of cake or come to study in the weekdays. No reason to get any less than 5/5 kind beans.

Location
Café Harepus stands proud on Studsgade in the middle of the Latin Quarter in the center of Aarhus. You will recognise it by it’s red walls and a cute bunny logo on the windows. Can’t say more than location couldn’t be much better 5/5 beans.

Kindness
Here goes the kindness scale. This is how we find out how much the business, we’re are potentially supporting cares about their impact on the world. Well big giant plus is that everything they serve is vegan. Definitely a win for the animals and the planet. And let’s not forget about all those poor sweet tooth vegans that haven’t had a croissant in a year because you just can’t find one anywhere! But if I’m being serious a completely vegan café and bakery is already a step that makes a huge difference.That’s solid 5/5 kind beans.

Summary
It’s a vegan café with coffee and pastries you’ve been dreaming about, what else you waiting for? Okay, in the Café Mellemfolk tour I said I will only mention this once but turned out Harepus had more to offer than I expected. So I think I need to mention that this place also has a nice focus on community. How about a free Sunday morning yoga? They host it every sunday at 9 in their backyard. I am yet to try it but to me it sounds lovely. Community: one extra kind bean for the effort.
